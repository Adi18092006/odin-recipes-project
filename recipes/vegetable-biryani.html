Vegetable Biryani

Description:

Vegetable Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made by layering spiced vegetables and partially cooked rice.
It's a popular Indian dish where long-grain basmati rice is infused with spices, herbs, and mixed vegetables
like carrots, peas, potatoes, and cauliflower. It's cooked on a low flame to allow the flavors to meld together perfectly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, cauliflower), diced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Drain soaked basmati rice and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil and ghee over medium heat. Add cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods, bay leaf, and cumin seeds. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add diced mixed vegetables and cook until slightly tender.
  5. Stir in garam masala, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Add drained basmati rice and stir to coat with the spices and vegetables.
  7. Transfer the rice mixture to a large pot. Add enough water to cover the rice by about 1 inch.
  8. Cover the pot and cook over low heat for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and all the water is absorbed.
  9. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing the rice with a fork.
  10. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves before serving.
