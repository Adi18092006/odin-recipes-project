Chicken Tikka Masala

Description:

Chicken Tikka Masala is a flavorful dish consisting of marinated and grilled chicken pieces
cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine with
tender chicken tikka immersed in a creamy, spiced gravy, perfect to pair with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger paste, and a mixture of garam masala, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and grill for about 20 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  3. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until soft and translucent.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Stir in diced tomatoes, garam masala, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add grilled chicken tikka to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
