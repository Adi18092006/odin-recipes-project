Palak Paneer

Description:

Palak Paneer is a popular Indian vegetarian dish made with spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese).
It's a nutritious and flavorful dish where the paneer cubes are cooked in a creamy spinach gravy
flavored with garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices. It's typically served with rice or Indian bread.

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 500g fresh spinach leaves, washed and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes, then drain and transfer to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and cook until they start to splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic, ginger paste, and chopped green chili. Cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
  6. Add the pureed spinach and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and heavy cream. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh cream or butter before serving.
