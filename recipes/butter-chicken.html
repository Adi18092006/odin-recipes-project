Butter Chicken

Description:

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish known for its creamy and mildly spiced tomato-based sauce.
Tender pieces of chicken are marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a rich sauce made with
butter, tomatoes, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, coriander, and turmeric.
It's typically served with rice or naan bread and garnished with fresh coriander leaves.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in yogurt for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until soft and translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and ginger paste, cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in garam masala, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  6. Pour in diced tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  7. Stir in heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
